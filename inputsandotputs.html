Inputs and Outputs: Reflections

Coding is completely new to me but I tried to familiarise myself with it before I arrived in Barcelona. I understood the logic behind it but I was not confident enough to apply it to things that I designed.

The pre-course helped to give a good understanding of the basics of code and how to use Arduino. I look forward to exploring more about this in the master's.
The circuits were easy to comprehend after they explained it to us and it made me want to use more of this as it would improve the scope with which I design. Josep and Adai were very helpful and showed me patiently until I learned about the Arduino that we were using (Barduino).

Design Dialogues

For the final exhibition of the dialogues, I prototyped my design space in AR and made an AR educational installation giving tips on sex education. At the exhibit, there was a physical intervention that induced discussion on common social stigma surrounding the body. I had a lot of fun designing the AR experience. The dialogues was a good chance to talk about my projects with people who are in various fields inside and outside design. It was a good way to learn how people can use and see my work depening on their exposure to the field of design. I got incredible feedback from my teachers and peers about how I can move forward with figuring out my space. The format of the dialogues was comfortable and relaxed while being able to show all the cool stuff we have been doing!
